Project - Fleet Management

Description - 
It is an online fleet service. It handles all the 
online booking of fleet services by customer and 
there details. Accessories are available has value
 added services. Customer can login to the site 
for booking as well as modification, even without 
login customer can modify. Login is there for 
employee who has to check the booking and a lot 
the car to a customer. Once the service ends 
customer can check the receipt online by logging 
to the account.
